## Verified ratings vs design stress

| Parameter | Datasheet (BD139/140) | Design worst case | Margin |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| VCEO | 80 V | 30 V rail-to-rail (±15 V) | 2.7x |
| IC continuous | 1.5 A | ~65 mA pk (verified sim, 8R tank) | 23x |
| ICM peak | 3 A | ~65 mA pk | 46x |
| PTOT @ Tc 25 C | 12.5 W | <0.7 W/device (sim) | 18x (case-referenced) |
| Tj max | 150 C | see thermal note | — |

- SOA at the operating point: VCE ≤ 15 V, IC ≤ 65 mA → ~1 W instantaneous,
  bounded by the dissipation limit, far inside the DC SOA envelope for a
  12.5 W / 1.5 A / 80 V device; second-breakdown limiting only appears at
  much higher VCE. Consistent with Figure 2 at a glance; exact curve
  read-off: needs-human-check (graphic).
- **hFE bin confirmed:** -16 group = 100–250 at 150 mA — matches the bias
  network assumption from the pre-order double-check (item 8). Order the
  `-16` suffix parts specifically.

## Thermal note (free-air)

Rth j-amb is 100 C/W: at 0.7 W free-standing, Tj ≈ ambient + 70 C — inside a
warm enclosure that approaches ~110 C. Within the 150 C limit but warm;
a small clip-on TO-126 heatsink or chassis bolting (Rth j-case is only
10 C/W) is cheap insurance and recommended at assembly. Bench-check case
temperature at the P-2 idle-bias step.
